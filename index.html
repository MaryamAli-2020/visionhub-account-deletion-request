Request account deletion

VisionHub users can request deletion of their account and associated application data at any time.

Delete your account in the app

  1. Open VisionHub and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Choose Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

Request deletion without app access

If you cannot access the app, email the VisionHub support contact using the link below. Include the email address or username connected to the account so the request can be verified.
